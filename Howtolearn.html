FALUN DAFA
9-DAY WORKSHOP (Free)

The 9-day Meditation class is held monthly and teaches a systematic series of Falun Dafa lectures in nine (9) consecutive days followed by hands-on exercise instructions and demonstration.

The class offers great benefits to participants, including but are not limited to

• Relieve physical & emotional stress
• Gain better health and inner harmony
• Enhance wisdom and enlightenment
